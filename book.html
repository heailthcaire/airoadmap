The Executive's AI Roadmap

How to Adopt, Scale, and Govern AI with Confidence

By Alain Trottier and Adam Mayer

About the Book

While 87% of executives call AI a strategic priority, 73% of AI initiatives fail to scale beyond pilot stage—trapped in "pilot purgatory" by unclear ROI, fragmented governance, and compliance fears.

The Executive's AI Roadmap provides the first comprehensive playbook to transform AI from scattered experiments into a measurable, board-ready portfolio that drives competitive advantage while maintaining regulatory compliance.

This isn't just another business book—it's a complete executive toolkit featuring 100+ downloadable templates, governance frameworks, and implementation guides created by leaders who have successfully scaled AI initiatives.

Publication Details

Proven Frameworks
